I am currently a Ph.D. student at [SSLAB](https://sslab.skku.edu) (System Security Lab), Department of
Computer Science and Engineering at [Sungkyunkwan University](https://www.skku.edu), advised by
[Hojoon Lee](https://hojoon-lee.github.io/).

## Research Interests

My research interests include, but are not limited to:

- Software testing (fuzzing, sanitizers, etc.)
- Hardware-accelerated security (e.g., ARM PAC, MTE)
- Programming languages
- Compilers for security
- Systems security (kernel, hypervisor)

More detailed information is available on the [CV](/cv/) page and my
[Google Scholar](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=AjgaSMoAAAAJ) profile.
